A chef shares his exclusive recipe to cook curry chicken
    Ingredients
  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves
      • Cooking steps
  • Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant.
  • Add in chicken.
  • Continue tos saute until chicken releases oil.
  • Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
  • Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  • Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  • Remove and serve.
