About Us

We are world's largest University

NorthSea University is a leading not-for-profit private university that takes pride in its uncompromising commitment to education through excellent academic and industry-driven programs. Ranked among the top 1.5% of higher education institutions in Asia, the University offers an engaging and holistic learning experience for students that will give them an edge in their future career paths.

The programs offered at NorthSea University are fully recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) and the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA). NorthSea University also works in collaboration with some of the finest institutions in the World.

At the University, students are immersed in an intellectually stimulating atmosphere where accepted knowledge is challenged, creative teaching methods are celebrated and professional networking opportunities are encouraged. The rich culture inculcated at the University can therefore ensure that students blossom into academically and socially exceptional young adults who will make meaningful contributions to society.
