Foundation Programmes & Pre-University

Foundation in Arts

Course Detials

Synopsis

The Foundation in Arts is a 1-year full time intensive comprehensive program designed to provide students with the necessary knowledge and learning essential for the smooth transition to undertake an undergraduate degree at NSU.

With the flexible program structure, students may complete their Foundation programs at their desired pace of up to two years. There are various courses that will enhance students' academic knowledge, expose students to the latest developments in their respective areas of study and foster their critical thinking skills. Simultaneously, it also sharpens students' presentation skills and English language proficiency.
