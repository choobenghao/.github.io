Programmes

At NSU, you can be confident that you will graduate with a recognized qualification!

All of our programs have been approved by the Malaysian Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE),
and most of them have received full accreditation from the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA).
We ensure the quality of our programs by collaborating effectively with our external examiners,
industry leaders, and relevant professional bodies.

Foundation Programmes &
Pre-University

The Foundation Programme is a one-year pre-university qualification designed to prepare SPM students and O-Levels equivalents for undergraduate studies at NSU.

Undergraduate &
Bachelor's Degree

With a recognised qualification by NSU, you can easily enter the workforce or continue your study locally or abroad.
NorthSea University has collaborated with our international partner universities to confer our students with postgraduate degrees from two universities through a mutual credit recognition arrangement.

Global Campuses

To serve a large portion of the populace, NSU has three campuses, namely the NSU Campus in Kuala Lumpur Malaysia,
NSU Campus in London UK, and the NSU Campus in Tokyo Japan. The two campuses house 12 faculties, 6 institutes, and 9 centers of study.

Our Facilities

Our campuses provide latest facilities for the academic and personal development of our staff and students.

School Library

library with a total built-up space of over 100,000 square feet and 295,000 volumes of books, publications, and numerous online research.

Gymnasium and Sports Complex

Students can use the well-equipped gymnasium at both campuses. All Campuses have the Sports Complex for basketball, futsal, netball, tennis, volleyball, and badminton. Students may use off-campus facilities such as the swimming pool and squash courts at nominal fees.

School Cafeteria

Cafeterias on our campuses offer a variety of food at reasonable prices and a comfortable ambiance for having meals.
